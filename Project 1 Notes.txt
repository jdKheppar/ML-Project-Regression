Batch Learning vs Online Learning
Batch Learning is when we have data and we train our model on the data while online learning is when our data is constantly changing and new data is being accumulated.
Online learning is used in scenarios like spam detection.

When we work in large organizations or research centers, we might need to work in VMs, that might require us to create Virtual Environment but usually python is installed in them.
